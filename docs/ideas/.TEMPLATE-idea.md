## Instructions

This document is a thinking framework. It moves from divergent (opening up) to convergent (closing down). Work the phases in order — the early ones expand your thinking, the later ones narrow it.

**For humans:** Work through each phase below. The early phases should feel messy and expansive. The later phases should feel sharp and decisive. If you're stuck, skip ahead and come back.

**For AI agents:** Read `docs/5day/ai/feynman-method.md` for the full protocol. In Phases 1–4 your job is to open up thinking — suggest angles the user hasn't considered, challenge assumptions, resist early convergence. In Phases 5–8 your job is to close down — sharpen the bet, stress-test it, cut scope.
